Dr.A.P.J Abdul Kalam

The man who inspired a billion lives

  • 1931- Born in Rameshwaram, Tamilnadu,India

  • 1954- Graduated from Saint Joseph's College Tiruchirapalli.

  • 1955- enrolled at MIT to study aerospace engineering

  • 1960- Joined DRDO as Chief Scientist

  • 1965- Started work rocket project independently at DRDO

  • 1969- Transferred to the ISRO

  • 1980- Developed AGNI and PRITVI

  • 1981- Padma Bhushan

  • 1990- Padma Vibushan

  • 1997- Bharat Ratna Government of India

  • 2002-07- Served as 11th President of India

  • 2012- Launced a mission for youth of India"What Can I Give Movement"

  • 2015- Passes away in Shillong,Meghalaya

"If you want to shine like sun, first burn like a sun" -A.P.J Abdul Kalam
